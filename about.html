Our History

Uganda Police 22nd Intake Fraternity SACCO Ltd was established to provide financial empowerment, unity, and support among officers of the 22nd intake. What began as a simple idea to encourage saving among members has grown into a structured cooperative with defined services and leadership, playing a critical role in improving the financial well-being of its members.

Our Mission

To promote the financial welfare of our members through savings, affordable loans, and investment opportunities, while fostering a spirit of solidarity, trust, and discipline among Uganda Police 22nd intake officers.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and transformative SACCO in Uganda's police force, recognized for financial excellence, transparency, and empowering our members towards a secure and dignified future.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our operations.
  • Transparency: We ensure openness and accountability in managing members' funds.
  • Discipline: We maintain order, respect, and responsibility among all members.
  • Unity: We foster a strong bond and collaboration among members of the 22nd intake.
  • Service: We prioritize members’ needs and deliver quality financial services.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to our SACCO's goals and long-term success.

Membership

1. Who Can Join?

Membership is open exclusively to serving and retired police officers of the Uganda Police Force’s 22nd intake who agree to abide by the SACCO’s constitution, policies, and guidelines. Members must also commit to active participation in SACCO activities and financial contributions.

2. Why Become a Member?

Becoming a member offers financial empowerment through savings, access to low-interest loans, share ownership, and the ability to benefit from collective growth. It also fosters unity, trust, and mutual support among officers of the 22nd intake.

3. How to acquire Membership?

To become a registered member of this SACCO, one Must:

  1. Be an officer from the Uganda Police 22nd Intake.
  2. Fill and submit a Membership Application Form.
  3. Pay a one-time Registration fee of UGX 20,000 (Non-refundable).
  4. Purchase the required minimum starting shares, 2 (Two) each valued at UGX 20,000.
  5. Make or be willing to make the required monthly savings contributions, a minimum of UGX 20,000.

4. Responsibilities of Members

  • Regularly saving according to SACCO policy
  • Participating in meetings and decision-making processes
  • Respecting the SACCO’s constitution and regulations
  • Repaying loans responsibly and on time
  • Promoting the interests and values of the SACCO

5. Rights and Privileges of a Member

  • Right to vote and be voted for in SACCO elections
  • Access to loan facilities and financial services
  • Right to request information and reports
  • Benefit from dividends and share earnings
  • Right to participate in SACCO activities and events
